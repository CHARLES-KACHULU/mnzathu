Place of Birth and Early Life

I was born in Thyolo, Malawi, in a vibrant and close-knit Goliati community. I grew up in a family of three brothers. My mother raised us on her own, teaching us the values of respect, resilience, and hard work. My childhood was full of outdoor games, exploration, and learning through curiosity. Family gatherings and cultural events shaped my early understanding of heritage and community. School was a place of discovery, where I cultivated my love for learning and developed a strong sense of discipline and responsibility.

Food I Love to Eat

Food is a source of comfort and joy. I enjoy traditional Malawian dishes like Nsima with chicken stew, fresh vegetables, and spicy relishes. Grilled fish from Lake Malawi is a favorite. Cooking allows me to experiment with flavors and create memorable experiences with loved ones.

My Beliefs and Philosophy About Life

I believe that life is a continuous journey of growth and learning. Challenges are opportunities to strengthen character, and empathy and honesty guide my interactions. I am committed to helping others and giving back to the community. Gratitude, mindfulness, and optimism are essential to living a fulfilled life.

Career and Professional Life

I have developed a career in education and business technology, focusing on innovative solutions for schools. I have designed online school report systems, automated student ranking, and developed apps for school finance management. My work combines my passion for technology and education, allowing me to positively impact both teachers and students.

Innovation: Mnzathu EduReport

Mnzathu EduReport is my innovative online school report system that simplifies result management for teachers, students, and parents, offering fast, secure, and real-time access.

Key Features:
• Teacher-specific login.
• Students and parents access results anytime.
• Automatic grading and ranking.
• Standardized and printable reports.
• Cumulative performance tracking.

Mnzathu EduReport makes school reports seamless, secure, and accessible to all.
